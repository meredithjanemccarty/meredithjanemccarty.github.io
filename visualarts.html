Overview

I have always enjoyed photography, and primarily shoot film. Most shots are from my trusty Konika Autoreflext T. An incredibly fun camera that i've since recommended to many friends is the Minolta P's series for interesting point-and-shoot film with a cool wide format look.

I'm using this site as a digital place for these photos to live. I will perhaps expand this to include other art forms, who knows?
